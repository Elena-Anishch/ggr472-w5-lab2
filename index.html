Elena's First Webpage

Inspired by 'The Latin Beat of Toronto' StoryMap created for DHU236 course, this website is developed as part of academic exercise for GGR472 course at the University of Toronto.

The story map includes information about salsa dance studios, lounges, festivals, and much more.

This website contains a GeoJSON file of dance studios and lounges listed in the story map.

Click on the buttons below to view the GeoJSON file and the Story Map.
